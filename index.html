Multi-Instrumentalist, Producer, Musician, DJ, and Entrepreneur. He has written songs for himself, other musicians, and bands. He has also produced songs for other artists and bands. He has performed live shows with various artists including I-nesta, Fausto, Eybi, Danny Yash, El Twox and others. Panamanian born and raised, playing the guitar for over 20 years now. He started at age 5 with his grandfather's acoustic guitar and learned how to play by ear. His father was a guitarist and played in bands and taught him how to play guitar. At age 10 he began learning how to play electric guitar and bass. By age 12 he had already been playing professionally for 2 years on the school band and the rest is story to tell.
