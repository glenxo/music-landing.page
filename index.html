Biography


    Multi-Instrumentalist, Producer, Musician, DJ, and    Entrepreneur. He has    written songs for himself, other    musicians, and bands. Glenn has also    produced songs for    other artists and bands. He has performed live shows    with    various artists including I-nesta, Fausto, Eybi, Danny Yash, El    Twox    and others. Panamanian born and raised, his       grandfather's acoustic   guitar and learned how to play by    ear.    His father was a guitarist and played   in bands and    taught him how to play guitar. At age 5 he began learning   how    to play electric guitar and bass. By age 12 he had already been    playing professionally for 2 years on the school band and the rest is story    to tell.
